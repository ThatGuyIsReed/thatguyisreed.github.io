Unhealthy Habits

Do you drink energy drinks? Do people call them unhealthy? Is it a habit? Want Energy drinks in minecraft? Look no farther than Unhealthy Habits.

Description

This mod adds craftable energy drinks which gives special effects when you drink them.

Drinks

Monster

Big energy boost! Speed and jump boost.

Redbull

Gives you wings, flings you into the air with levitation.
